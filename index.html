1. Match Types aur Prize Ka Tarika
• Bargunda (Bermuda) Matches (Duo aur Squad): Yeh total 6 matches honge. Sab matches ka total points count hoga. Jo player/team ke 6 matches ke baad sabse zyada points banayega, usko hi prize milega (Top 1 sabse bada, fir Top 2, Top 3).
• CS, Lone Wolf, aur Solo Matches: Har match bilkul alag hai. Sirf us match mein Booyah (1st place) karne wale ko prize milega.

2. Point System (Sab matches mein same)
• Booyah (1st place) karne pe: +10 points
• Har kill pe: +1 point
• Bargunda series mein sab 6 matches ke points add hote jayenge → final total points se Top 1/2/3 decide hoga.
• CS/Lone Wolf/Solo mein points sirf us match ke liye (prize Booyah winner ko).

3. Prize Pool
• Bargunda (Duo/Squad series): Full prize pool 6 matches ke total points ke basis pe Top 1, Top 2, Top 3 ko.
• CS / Lone Wolf / Solo: Har match ka prize alag (sirf Booyah winner ko) – match card pe dekho.

4. Registration Kaise Kare (Important!)
1. Match choose karo → Apna/Team ka name bharo.
2. Entry fee UPI se pay karo.
3. Payment ka screenshot zaroor upload karo (modal mein) – bina screenshot ke join nahi hoga.
4. Screenshot upload hone ke baad hi registration confirm hoga.
5. Main tujhe private WhatsApp group mein add kar dunga.
6. Match se 15 minute pehle group mein Room ID + Password bhej dunga.
7. Group mein sirf registered players rahenge – bina payment ke entry nahi milegi.

5. Important Baatein
• Fair play rakho – cheating pakde gaye to permanent ban + prize nahi milega.
• Real name / in-game ID use karo.
• Result aur winner list WhatsApp channel pe update honge.
• Koi problem ho to Support button pe message kar dena.

Sabse badi baat: Payment screenshot upload karna compulsory hai – warna group nahi milega!
